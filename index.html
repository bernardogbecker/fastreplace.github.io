The Solution

How Does WEG FastReplace Work?

We turn a complex engineering problem into something as simple as taking photos. The process uses your own smartphone to guarantee perfect interchangeability.

1

Smartphone Capture

Using a partner app, you take photos of the motor and installation area. No expensive scanners or lengthy technical visits are needed.

2

3D Model Creation

The photos are processed by a photogrammetry algorithm, creating a reliable 3D model (>98% accuracy).

3

Design Specifications

On the platform, you will be able to measure any dimensions that are critical to the project.

4

Solution Delivery

Engineering teams will prioritize your offer, ensuring speed and interchangeability of the proposed solution.

WEG FastReplace applies to any equipment size, from a small washing machine motor, for example, to a huge turbo generator.
